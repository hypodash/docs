---
title: "Generate Ideas"
description: "Stuck on what to write?"
---

Use AI to find relevant ideas to your company or product and start creating content with little effort.

<Info>
  **Using your OpenAi key with Hypodash will consume tokens. You will be billed seperately by OpenAi outside your Hypodash plan. The Ideas feature will consume tokens for each generation.**
</Info>

1. Navigate to [Ideas](https://hypodash.com/ideas) and enter a keyword relating to your topic or keyword.
1. Click `Generate` and wait for 10 new article ideas to be created.
1. If you spot an idea you like, click the `Write` button and it'll prefill the `Article Create` form.
